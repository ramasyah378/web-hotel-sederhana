About Us

Over the last 25 years, the Blue Hotels organisation has been known for dependably providing the best Indonesia hospitality experience. It combines modern style and comfort with the warmth of Old World hospitality. With more than 50 hotels and resorts across the world, it is one of the world's largest hotel chains. We believe in the values of Indian hospitality, and our crew is our most valuable asset, providing passionate and memorable hospitality to everyone we meet.
